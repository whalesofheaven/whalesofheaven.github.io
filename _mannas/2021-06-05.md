A person’s wisdom yields patience;
    it is to one’s glory to overlook an offense.
A king’s rage is like the roar of a lion,
    but his favor is like dew on the grass.

왕의 노함은 사자의 부르짖음 같고 그의 은택은 풀 위에 이슬 같으니라
